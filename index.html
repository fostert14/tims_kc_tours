Underground Tours

$45 per person or $200 for five passengers (1 hour)

Learn about the mining process and how the space is transformed into industrial buildings. Roughly 30 million square feet of leasable industrial space is located in several Kansas City undergrounds. Discover the hidden world beneath Kansas City's streets and explore the fascinating history of these subterranean spaces.

Concierge Tours

$300 for six hours for up to five people

Tim will drive you to various historical sites and provide narratives for various elements of Kansas City's history. You are in charge of the destinations. Tim will chauffeur your party to the Liberty Memorial, Union Station, City Market, Fort Osage, Jesse James boyhood home, St. Joseph sites (Pony Express, Jesse James murder house, etc.), driving tour of the Battle of Westport, Pendergast era and mafia rule, overland trails, or any combination thereof.
