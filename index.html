Underground Tours

$45 per person or $200 for five passengers (1 hour)

Learn about the mining process and how the space is transformed into industrial buildings. Roughly 30 million square feet of leasable industrial space is located in several Kansas City undergrounds. Discover the hidden world beneath Kansas City's streets and explore the fascinating history of these subterranean spaces.

Highly recommend Monday through Friday during normal business hours.

Concierge Tours

$300 for six hours for up to five people

Tim will drive you to various historical sites and provide narratives for various elements of Kansas City's history. You are in charge of the destinations. Tim will chauffeur your party to the Liberty Memorial, Union Station, City Market, Fort Osage, Jesse James boyhood home, St. Joseph sites (Pony Express, Jesse James murder house, etc.), driving tour of the Battle of Westport, Pendergast era and mafia rule, overland trails, or any combination thereof..

Travel in comfort in Tim's new, clean 6-passenger SUV. This is an intimate tour experience designed for small groups.

About Tim

Tim is a semi-retired commercial real estate broker with a background and passion for history. He holds a degree in history and political science as well as a masters in public administration. Somehow his education led him to real estate.

Raised in a small town in Southern Illinois, Tim grew up working in his parents' feedstore. Although not directly related to his tours, this upbringing is the source of many, many anecdotes that he may share with his customers.

Tim's nearly three decade career in real estate has given him an extensive knowledge of the Kansas City area. Combined with his love of history, a tour with Tim will provide you an intimate look at Kansas City
